## About Me

I'm an AI/ML engineer focused on building intelligent, data-driven systems that solve real-world problems at the intersection of machine learning and quantitative finance. My work spans the full spectrum — from exploratory research and model development to production-grade deployment.

My core expertise includes **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **Quantitative Finance**, **Predictive Analytics**, **Time Series Forecasting**, and **Algorithmic Trading**. I design and implement end-to-end AI pipelines that take ideas from research notebooks to live, production-ready systems.
